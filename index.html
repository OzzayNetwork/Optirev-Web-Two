Agency banking

2011-2013
02. -

Description

The agency banking solution delivers financial inclusion for unbanked and under-banked customers who live in remote locations without access to brick and mortar branches.

By increasing the number of touch points providing service access, whilst reducing transaction costs. The agency banking model is a key part of the financial inclusion strategy for any continent.

Robust, sustainable, agile The solution allows customers to access basic banking transactions at a location convenient to them. The Agent could be located in a convenience store outlet, or they can travel to the customer. Equipped with a point-of-sale (pos) card reader and ussd enabled devices, which are sim enabled, they can remotely and securely connect to the server. Customers transact using a card, mobile phone or mobile wallet. Increasing the reach of services through agents enables the population to access services without creating additional risk and cost.
